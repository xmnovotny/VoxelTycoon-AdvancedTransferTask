[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*]Allows you to set the percent of vehicle's units capacity as a limit for the maximum amount of cargo loaded or the amount of cargo remaining after unloading.
[*][b]NEW[/b] option to wait for full load/unload of any cargo type
[/list]

[h2]Usage[/h2]
Just click on the percentage text in the loading/unloading task in the vehicle schedule.
In you want to disable limit, leave the field empty or set it to the [b]100[/b] for loading task and the [b]0[/b] for unloading task.
[b]Full[/b] option in the task behaves similarly - when it is checked, vehicle will wait until loading/unloading limit is reached.
For set wait for full load/unload any cargo type, click at "FULL" toggle and chose desired option from the menu.
