[h2]Features[/h2]

Allows you to set the percent of vehicle's units capacity as a limit for the maximum amount of items loaded or the amount of items remaining after unloading.

[h2]Usage[/h2]
Just click on the percentage text in the loading/unloading task in the vehicle schedule.
In you want to disable limit, leave the field empty or set it to the [b]100[/b] for loading task and the [b]0[/b] for unloading task.
[b]Full[/b] option in the task behaves similarly - when it is checked, vehicle will wait until loading/unloading limit is reached.

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
It should be compatible with with the majority of mods
[b][url=steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2498615476]Transport capacity estimator & logistics calculator[/url][/b] has a compatibility for calculation with the limits made by this mod
